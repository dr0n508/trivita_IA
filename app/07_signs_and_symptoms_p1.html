Knowledge is power when it comes to recognizing silent inflammation. Noticing some of these symptoms is your first step to reversing inflammation and achieving wellness, followed by taking appropriate ACTION. This is vital to reduce your incidence of developing chronic inflammatory disease in your lifetime

Signs and Symptoms (1/2)

Please select (choose one) the level for each symptom below.

1. Anxiety (hyper-awareness of circumstances beyond your control)

2. Depression (hyper-awareness of feeling helpless/powerless)

3. Insomnia (Less than 7 hours of uninterrupted sleep each night, difficulty falling asleep, difficulty staying asleep)

4. Excessive sleep (More than 9 hours each night)

5. Poor concentration (Easily distracted and or poor focus, attention and/or comprehension)

6. Mood swings (Mood triggers such as anger, melancholy, lonely, sadness, irritable, seasonal mood swings, etc)

7. Memory loss (Unable to remember things like you used to)
